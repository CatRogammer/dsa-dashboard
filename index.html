#️⃣ Open Addressing (Closed Hashing)

All elements are stored in the hash table itself. On collision, probe for next slot.

Linear: h(k,i) = (h(k) + i) mod m  |  Quadratic: h(k,i) = (h(k) + i²) mod m  |  Double: h(k,i) = (h₁(k) + i·h₂(k)) mod m
